French electronic music duo formed in Paris in 1993 by Guy—Manuel de Homem—Christo and Thomas Bangalter. They achieved popularity in the late 1990s as part of the French house the movement they also had success in the years following, They have worn ornate helmets and gloves—suits to assume robot personas .

After Bangalter and Homem-Christo’s indie rock band Darlin’ disbanded, they began experimenting with drum machines Their debut studio — album Homework was released by Virgin Records in 1997 to positive reviews backed by singles Around the World and Da Funk Their second second album, Discovery had further success around the globe.
